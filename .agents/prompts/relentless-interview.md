# Relentless Interview Prompt

Interview me persistently with targeted follow-up questions until we reach shared understanding.

Workflow reference:

- `.agents/skills/relentless-interview/SKILL.md`

Requirements:

- Ask one focused question at a time, building on my responses.
- Follow the direction my answers take—don't follow a preset list.
- Persist until we both articulate the same mental model.
- Surface unstated constraints and hidden assumptions.
- Remain collaborative and respectful throughout.

Output format:

1. Shared understanding synthesized in your words
1. My confirmation that it matches my intent
1. Documented clarity for reference
